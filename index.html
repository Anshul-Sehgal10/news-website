New Study Reveals Alarming Rise in Mental Health Issues

Owl be damned! New research finds that being a night owl is bad for your mental health. Going to bed by 1 a.m. can lower your risk of developing mental and behavioral conditions such as depression and anxiety, according to a study published last month in the journal Psychiatry Research. Researchers compared the preferred sleep timing, known as chronotype, with the actual sleep behavior of nearly 74,000 UK adults.
